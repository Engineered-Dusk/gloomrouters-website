Gloom Routers

Route the light. Survive the dark.

Gloom Routers is a dark sci-fi tactical card/board game about rival expedition machines fighting over engineered light corridors in a ruined world.

What it is

Command Mechs, contest Relics, and push through the Corridor before the Gloom closes in. Gloom Routers focuses on tactical pathing, high-pressure engagements, and board-state control through cards.

World & theme

The world of Gloom Routers is built from damaged industrial infrastructure, routed-light channels, and expedition hardware operating at the edge of visibility. Every route is temporary. Every corridor can fail.
